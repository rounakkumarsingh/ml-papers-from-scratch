# Blogging Guidelines

## What blogs are
- Thoughtful synthesis of understanding
- Explanations in your own words
- Honest about confusion and limitations

## What blogs are not
- Paper summaries
- Paraphrased abstracts
- Tutorial rewrites

## When to blog
- After intuition stabilizes
- After implementation reveals friction
- After discovering a non-obvious insight

## Tone
- Precise
- Honest
- Non-promotional

## Minimum bar
Every blog must answer at least one question
you could not answer before reading the paper.
